Oxford Elbow Score (OES)

1. During the past 4 weeks... Have you had difficulty lifting things in your home, such as putting out the rubbish, because of your elbow problem?

2. During the past 4 weeks... Have you had difficulty carrying bags of shopping, because of your elbow problem?

3. During the past 4 weeks... Have you had any difficulty washing yourself all over, because of your elbow problem?

4. During the past 4 weeks... Have you had any difficulty dressing yourself, because of your elbow problem?

5. During the past 4 weeks... Have you felt that your elbow problem is “controlling your life”?

6. During the past 4 weeks... How much has your elbow problem been “on your mind”?

7. During the past 4 weeks... Have you been troubled by pain from your elbow in bed at night?

8. During the past 4 weeks... How often has your elbow pain interfered with your sleeping?

9. During the past 4 weeks... How much has your elbow problem interfered with your usual work or everyday activities?

10. During the past 4 weeks... Has your elbow problem limited your ability to take part in leisure activities that you enjoy doing?

11. During the past 4 weeks... How would you describe the worst pain you have from your elbow?

12. During the past 4 weeks... How would you describe the pain you usually have from your elbow?
